How it works

Dead simple.

From first hand to fully settled in three steps. No math, no spreadsheets.

Step 01

Create your group

Name your crew and share the 6-character invite code. Players join instantly — no account required. Guests appear on the leaderboard the moment the session is logged.

Step 02

Log the session

The admin enters each player's total buy-in amount and their final chip count at the end of the game. PokerApp does the rest — net profit, losses, and chip mismatch warnings are all calculated automatically.

Step 03

Review & settle

The Settlement screen shows the optimised transaction list: exactly who pays whom and how much. One tap opens Venmo or CashApp with the amount pre-filled. Done before you leave the table.

Step 04

Leaderboard updates live

The moment a session is confirmed, every player's stats update in real time. The leaderboard, player profiles, earnings chart, and Hall of Fame all reflect the new data instantly.
